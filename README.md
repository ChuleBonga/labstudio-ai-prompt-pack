## How to use
1. Open any `.md` file.
2. Copy a prompt, replace the `{placeholders}` (e.g. `{product}`, `{audience}`).
3. Paste into your AI chat. Iterate - ask it to 'make it shorter', 'change tone to bold', etc.

## Tips
- Keep a `Favorites.md` of prompts you reuse.
- For best results, give concrete specifics in placeholders (avoid vague `{topic}`).
- Combine a Marketing prompt with a Productivity-Automation prompt to ship faster.
